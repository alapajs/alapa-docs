## Become a Sponsor

At Alapa, we believe in the power of community and collaboration. If you share our vision of transforming web development and wish to support the ongoing development of Alapa, consider becoming a sponsor. Your generous contribution will directly help us accelerate development, enhance the framework's features, improve documentation, and ensure Alapa remains a top-tier choice for developers worldwide.

### Why Sponsor Alapa?

Your sponsorship isn’t just an investment in a framework — it's a commitment to shaping the future of modern web development. Whether you're an individual, a business, or a company, your support will help us provide the tools that empower developers to create scalable, maintainable, and innovative applications.

### Sponsorship Benefits

As a sponsor, you’ll enjoy the following exclusive benefits:

- **Brand Visibility**: Your company or personal brand will be featured prominently in Alapa’s documentation and promotional materials, giving you exposure to a global audience of developers.
- **Acknowledgment**: We’ll proudly acknowledge your contribution in Alapa’s release notes, updates, and official communications, showcasing your role in the framework’s growth.
- **Influence**: As a sponsor, you’ll have a voice in guiding the future of Alapa. You’ll be able to influence the prioritization of new features and improvements that align with your needs and vision.

### Sponsorship Plans

We offer a range of sponsorship plans to suit different levels of support and involvement:

#### **Bronze Sponsor**

- Contribution: $5 per month
- **Benefits**:
  - Acknowledgment in the Alapa documentation
  - Recognition in release notes and official communications
  - Thank-you email from the team

#### **Silver Sponsor**

- Contribution: $25 per month
- **Benefits**:
  - All benefits of the Bronze level
  - Your logo featured in Alapa’s documentation and website
  - Exclusive access to beta releases and early feature previews
  - Influence on upcoming feature priorities

#### **Gold Sponsor**

- Contribution: $50 per month
- **Benefits**:
  - All benefits of the Silver level
  - Prominent placement of your logo on Alapa’s homepage and promotional materials
  - Direct communication with the development team for feature requests or collaborations
  - Invitation to exclusive sponsor events and meetups

### One-Time or Custom Sponsorship

If you wish to support Alapa without committing to a monthly plan, you can make a **one-time contribution** or sponsor at a **custom amount**. Whether it's a single contribution or an ongoing but flexible support arrangement, we welcome all forms of generosity.

Your support, regardless of the amount, will be acknowledged and deeply appreciated.

To make a one-time or custom contribution, visit [Buy Me a Coffee](https://buymeacoffee.com/alapajs) and select the amount that works best for you.
