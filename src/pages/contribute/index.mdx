## Contributing

Alapa thrives because of the amazing community of developers who contribute to its growth and improvement. Whether you're an experienced developer or just getting started, your contributions are always welcome!

We invite you to collaborate, report bugs, or share your ideas for new features. By working together, we can make Alapa an even more powerful and flexible framework for everyone.

### How to Contribute

- **Join the Community**: Connect with fellow developers on our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/nPqTeJ8SgK) server. It's a great place to ask questions, collaborate on ideas, and share your experiences using Alapa.
- **Open Issues**: If you encounter any bugs or have ideas for new features, feel free to open an issue. We encourage you to report anything that can improve the framework.

- **Submit Pull Requests**: If you're ready to contribute code, we'd love to see your pull requests! Please ensure your contributions align with Alapa’s code style and structure.

Your input, feedback, and contributions are what help Alapa grow. Together, we can continue to build a framework that’s as powerful, flexible, and developer-friendly as possible.

Thank you for being a part of the Alapa community!
